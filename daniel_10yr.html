When Andrew was called Home to Jesus on June 25th 1997 we cut out a calendar clipping for that exact date just to add to our ‘Andrew memories’. This is what it says …

“Do not be afraid, Daniel. Since the first day that you set your mind to gain understanding and to humble yourself before your God, your words were heard”
Daniel 10 v 12.

Though called home before the answer comes, with clearer vision you may see how your heart-cry has been heard and answered in a way least expected.

Unanswered yet? No, do not say ungranted;

Perhaps your part is not yet wholly done;

The work began when first your prayer was uttered,

And God will finish what He has begun.
